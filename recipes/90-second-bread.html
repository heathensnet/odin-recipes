90 Second Bread

Incredibly simple bread in only 90 seconds

This used to be a CONSTANT in my house, and likely still would be if I owned a microwave. Experiment with different (microwave-safe) containers to mix things up, in the past I used everything from pretty ramekins to egg poachers, even cute non-metal cookie cutters on a plate. The original recipe called for a single egg, but I didn't use eggs at the time, so I've listed alternatives I have used while making this. The recipe is also very forgiving and easy to play around with.

Pro-tip: Replace the egg substitue with banana to get absolutely delicious banana bread. Throw some chocolate chips in if you want to really play it up.

Ingredients

  • 3tbsp almond flour
  • 1tbsp oil
  • 1/4tsp baking powder
  • 3tbsp aquafaba, a flax-egg (takes longer than 90 seconds though), 1 egg's worth of egg replacer, etc

Steps

  1. Mix well.
  2. Microwave for 90 seconds.
  3. It legit comes out roughly the temperature of the sun's surface every time, so let cool for one eternity.
